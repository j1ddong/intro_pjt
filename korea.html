KOREA

Language
The Korean language, known as Hangul, is unique and has its own writing system. It is spoken by the populations of both North and South Korea, although there are some differences in pronunciation and vocabulary between the two countries.
Historical Significance
The Korean Peninsula has a complex history marked by various kingdoms, invasions, and interactions with neighboring nations, such as China and Japan. It has faced numerous challenges, including colonization and conflict.
K-pop
South Korea has gained international prominence for its pop culture, especially K-pop (Korean pop music) and K-dramas (Korean television dramas). These cultural exports have a significant global fan base.
